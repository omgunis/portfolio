    2 Player Snake

    Fun twist on a classic game

    Play as a single player or with a friend!

    Instructions:


    Single player:

  • Use arrow keys to move and collect food
  • Don't touch the edges or run into yourself
  • Try your best to beat the high score

    • Two Player:
  • Use WASD keys and arrow keys to collect food
  • Don't touch edges or run into yourself
  • When a player collects food, they grow by 2 and oppenent shrinks by 1
